Community Standards

We take respect seriously.

After Hours is an exclusive space built on dignity and mutual respect. We hold every member to the same high standard.

Zero Harassment Tolerance

Unwanted advances, aggressive messaging, or any form of harassment results in immediate and permanent removal. No warnings, no second chances.

Treat Others with Dignity

Every member deserves to feel comfortable and respected. Rude, demeaning, or disrespectful behavior is not tolerated.

No Photo Sharing in Chat

For the safety of all members, photo and media sharing within messages is disabled. Connections are built on conversation first.

Be Who You Say You Are

Misrepresentation, catfishing, or impersonation of any kind is a permanent ban offense. Authenticity is the foundation of After Hours.

A space worth protecting.

After Hours is intentionally exclusive. We built this community for adults who want genuine, spontaneous connection — and that requires every member to show up with respect and good intentions.

Our moderation team reviews reports quickly. If something feels off, report it — we act fast.

Photo sharing is disabled by design. We made this decision intentionally to protect our members and maintain the integrity of the platform. All connections happen through text conversation.
